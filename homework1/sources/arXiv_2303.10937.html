Our demonstration reveals that a substantial amount of data is not necessary for estimating depth. Our analysis demonstrates that depth priors calculated using COCO exhibit similarities to those computed using PASCAL, as evident from Figure S.3. Furthermore, we mention in Sec. 4.3 that applying the depth priors calculated from COCO to Conceptual Captions (CC) results in a more significant performance improvement on the noisy CC compared to COCO, even though priors are computed from COCO.
